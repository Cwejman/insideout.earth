about us

Reality is shaped from within.

What we carry within us becomes the world we share. How we meet ourselves shapes how we meet each other — and ultimately, the societies we build together.

InsideOut is a purpose-driven organisation in Gothenburg that puts this insight into practice — through work that strengthens people's inner capacity alongside social participation and inclusion. The work is grounded in knowledge, creativity and relation.

domains

Three domains, one coherent whole.

Next Generation

Directed at children and families — the foundation of InsideOut's work.

  • Nova Klubb A stable programme with over 320 children and more than 200 families. Safe, inclusive spaces where children and families build relationships and develop social skills.
  • Bright Minds A targeted programme for children and young people who need support developing focus, self-regulation and emotional regulation.

Personal Evolution

Focus on individual development — understanding yourself, finding direction and turning insight into action.

  • Healing Beyond Fear Creates conditions for meeting and processing trauma, stress and past experiences.
  • The Shift Focuses on change in practice — how people can break ingrained thought and behaviour patterns.
  • Conscious Creator Integrates self-leadership, creative expression and agency — from insight to concrete action.

Connected Society

Focus on relationships between people — for more cohesive and inclusive societies.

  • Wisdom Wheels Activities where older and younger people and newcomers meet in shared experiences for exchange and deeper understanding.
  • Hero Journey An innovative game in both digital and analogue form that creates conditions for self-reflection and connection between individuals.
